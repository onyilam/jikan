About Jikan.

>> Learn more about the project and our goals.


Jikan is an online tool designed to support researchers to perform systematic literature reviews. The underlying algorithm recommends papers for review to researchers based on the similarity of papers.

The database currently hosts over 1 million papers that spans across seven different disciplines. In the next iteration of the project, we hope to allow researchers to upload a list of papers, and recommend related content to help them better understand the landscape of their field.
